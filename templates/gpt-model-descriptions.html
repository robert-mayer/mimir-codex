Model Descriptions

gpt-3.5-turbo

Fast and cost-effective. Best for following specific instructions and structured tasks.

  • Recommended for: Shorter responses, commands, and direct answers.
gpt-4

Advanced reasoning, highly accurate, and nuanced. Slower and more costly compared to other options.

  • Recommended for: Detailed storytelling, complex problem-solving, and deeper campaign lore.
gpt-4-turbo

Faster and more affordable than standard GPT-4, while still offering strong capabilities.

  • Recommended for: Most situations where good quality and reduced cost are important.
gpt-4o

Balanced version of GPT-4, optimized for reasonable cost and good performance.

  • Recommended for: High-volume usage where a blend of quality and efficiency is needed.
gpt-4o-mini

Optimized for fast, cost-effective responses, handling very high volumes efficiently.

  • Recommended for: High-traffic applications or situations where response time is critical, and depth is less important.
